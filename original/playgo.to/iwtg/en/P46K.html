4 black stones are in danger!
Kill thewhite stones before being killed!

Capture the 2 white stones touching the black ones.
Here, you can't capture white with one move.

If you can capture the white stone (D5) which is separating black stones, it will be an easy game.

[Tip] Don't try to attack the enemy directly.

Why do we have to capturethe opponent's stones?

It is because by removing opponent's stones that are separating your stones, your divided stones can be all connected.

This is very important aspect of Go.
Just remember to play so that all your stones connect each other whenever you can.
