Cutting stones

It looks as if white has the larger territory.

However, her wall has a weak point. Just "cut" white's wall.

This shows that it is possible to cut stones that are diagonally connected.

If stones are cut into two parts, each has to live separately - you will have a hard time.

Black has 30 points while white has 31 points. Thus it looks like white has a 1 point win.

However, the territory on the lower side is not all white's.

Just cut white's wall.

White could not save the two stones.
