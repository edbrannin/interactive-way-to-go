38 Kyu Problems - Fight in the corner

Near the corners of the board, you have fewer ways to escape and can be captured more easily.

Thus the variations tend to be more complex.

It is said that the mark of a Go wizard is being able to live the corners.

This is the Semeai situation in the corner.

Capture the white stone which is cutting black.

CAUTION: Play on until you see success or failed. Keep attacking even if white escapes.

Be careful from which way you make the Atari.
